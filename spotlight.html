Artist Spotlight

Welcome to the Artist Spotlight. Below you will find biographies of our featured artists.

James Quinn is a British singer-songwriter and producer known for his atmospheric blend of indie pop and electronic music. He is the artist behind the track "Le Carrousel," which showcases his emotive vocals and intricate production style. His music often combines dreamy melodies with introspective lyrics, creating an immersive listening experience. Quinn's unique sound and ability to evoke deep emotions through his music have earned him recognition in the indie music scene.

Kamaal Williams is a British jazz musician, producer, and multi-instrumentalist celebrated for his innovative fusion of jazz, funk, and electronic music. Emerging as a key figure in the UK’s contemporary jazz scene, he gained recognition as one-half of the duo Yussef Kamaal. Williams’ solo work explores groove-heavy compositions, blending traditional jazz elements with modern beats and a soulful touch. His unique sound has earned him acclaim for pushing the boundaries of jazz into fresh, genre-defying territories.

Knock2 is an American electronic music producer and DJ known for his distinctive blend of house, future bass, and bass music. He gained significant attention with his viral tracks, characterized by high-energy drops and catchy melodies. His music often features innovative sound design and heavy basslines, blending elements of hip-hop and electronic music. Knock2's unique style and bold approach to production have made him a rising star in the electronic music scene.

Soulwax is a Belgian electronic music duo formed by brothers David and Stephen Dewaele. Known for blending rock, electronic, and dance music, they have gained a reputation for their energetic and genre-defying sound. Their work spans albums, remixes, and live performances, often incorporating innovative and experimental elements. Soulwax's unique approach to music has made them influential figures in the electronic music scene, with a dedicated following worldwide.

Nfract is an electronic music producer known for their early work in dark ambient and depressive breakcore, creating intense and evocative soundscapes. In 2021, they announced plans to transition their focus toward rave music, signaling a new chapter in their creative journey. This shift reflects Nfract’s drive to explore fresh sonic territories while continuing to deliver innovative and boundary-pushing tracks.

Rispaa, the professional alias of Dante Anthony Taj Barnes, is an American electronic music producer known for their unique blend of ambient soundscapes and glitchy electronic beats. Born on September 4, 2000, in Minnesota, Rispaa began their journey into electronic music in 2017, drawing inspiration from artists like Feryquitous and Nhato. Their work is characterized by calm, immersive tones layered with intricate and dynamic rhythms, offering listeners a truly innovative experience. Rispaa’s debut EP, Intermission, released in 2022, showcases their dedication to pushing the boundaries of electronic music while maintaining an emotive and atmospheric quality.
